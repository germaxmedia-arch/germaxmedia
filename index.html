AGPO Registration

Unlock the 30% Government Procurement Preference

The Access to Government Procurement Opportunities (AGPO) programme reserves 30% of government tenders for businesses owned by Youth (18–35), Women, and Persons with Disability. We register you quickly and correctly.

  • Eligibility check and category confirmation
  • Online application and document upload
  • Certificate acquisition and renewal support

FAQs

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about eGP registration and tendering in Kenya — answered plainly.

The eGP (electronic Government Procurement) portal is the Government of Kenya's online system for purchasing goods and services. Any business that wants to supply to national government ministries and agencies must be registered on this portal. Without registration, you cannot access tender notices or submit bids for national government contracts.

Once we receive all your required documents, we typically complete registration within 24–48 business hours. The government portal itself may take 1–3 additional days to approve your account. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

The exact requirements depend on your business type, but commonly needed documents include: Certificate of Incorporation or Business Registration, KRA PIN Certificate, Tax Compliance Certificate, CR12 (for limited companies), National ID of directors, and company bank details. We'll send you a specific checklist once you contact us.

AGPO is available to businesses where the majority ownership (at least 70%) belongs to Youth (citizens aged 18–35), Women, or Persons with Disability. The business must also be registered in Kenya and have a valid Tax Compliance Certificate. Contact us and we'll confirm your eligibility quickly.
